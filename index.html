Who is Spider Man?

Spider Man is Peter Parker!

He was bitten by a radioactive spider and he was given powers. His uncle , Ben, was shot by a burgalar and Peter couldn't save him. The one thing he remembered what Ben taught him was, "With great power, comes great responsibility".

Now he uses his power under the mask of Spider Man to fight crime and protect people. he ocassionaly fights alongside The Avengers, The Fantastic 4 and The X-Men. But most of the time he is fighting villains like Venom, Dr.Octavius and Kingpin.

He was created by Stan Lee, the celebrated writer and architect of the Marvel Universe. His favorite character, Spider Man is one of the most recognized chracters in comic book history.

There is an upcoming film of Spider Man, who is acted out by Tom Holland, is called "Spider Man : Brand New Day" which is said to be the most hyped movie ever.
